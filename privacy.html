Privacy Policy - Saliim Lab

Saliim Lab is a personal, single-user tool developed and used solely by the account owner to upload their own short-form videos to their own TikTok account.

1. Data Collection

Saliim Lab does not collect, store, or share any personal data from third parties. The only credentials used are the owner's own TikTok OAuth access token, which is required to upload videos to the owner's own TikTok account.

2. Data Storage

OAuth tokens are stored locally on the owner's own device and are not transmitted to any external server. No analytics, tracking, or third-party SDKs are used.

3. Data Sharing

No data is shared with any third party. The tool communicates only with the official TikTok API endpoints.

4. User Rights

The owner can revoke access at any time via TikTok account settings. The owner can delete locally stored tokens at any time.
