Privacy Policy - kingxkal / Saliim Lab

Operator: kingxkal (Saliim Lab) · Site: https://kingxkal.github.io/saliim-lab/

Saliim Lab (operated by kingxkal) is a personal, single-user tool developed and used solely by the account owner (kingxkal) to upload their own short-form videos and images to their own social media accounts, including TikTok, Pinterest, YouTube, Instagram, Threads, Facebook, and X (Twitter).

1. Data Collection

Saliim Lab does not collect, store, or share any personal data from third parties. The only credentials used are the owner's own OAuth access tokens for the connected platforms (including TikTok, Pinterest, YouTube, Instagram, Threads, Facebook, and X), which are required to upload videos, images, and Pins to the owner's own accounts and boards.

2. Data Storage

OAuth tokens are stored locally on the owner's own device and are not transmitted to any external server. No analytics, tracking, or third-party SDKs are used.

3. Data Sharing

No data is shared with any third party. The tool communicates only with the official APIs of each connected platform, including the TikTok API, the Pinterest API (v5), the YouTube Data API, the Meta Graph API, and the X API.

4. User Rights

The owner can revoke access at any time via each platform's account settings (e.g., TikTok account settings, Pinterest app permissions at pinterest.com/settings/apps, and similar settings on other connected platforms). The owner can delete locally stored tokens at any time.
